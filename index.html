Our Commitment to Swahili Heritage

Mission & Vision

At MILA ZETU, we are dedicated to preserving, promoting, and celebrating the rich cultural heritage of the Swahili Coast through art, food, education, and community engagement.

  • Preserve traditional crafts like Kofia-making and pottery.
  • Teach the art of Swahili cuisine through culinary classes.
  • Empower youth through mentorship and scholarships.
  • Host festivals and workshops to unite communities.
  • Document and share Swahili history and traditions.
  • Foster global appreciation for Swahili culture.
