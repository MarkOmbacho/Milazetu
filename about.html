About Us

Our Story

MILA ZETU was founded in 2024 with a vision to preserve, promote, and celebrate the rich cultural heritage of the Swahili Coast. Our name, which means "Our Traditions" in Swahili, reflects our commitment to keeping these traditions alive for future generations.

From traditional crafts and culinary arts to youth empowerment and community engagement, we strive to create a global appreciation for Swahili culture.

Our Mission

To preserve, promote, and celebrate Swahili heritage through art, food, education, and community engagement.

Our Vision

A world where Swahili culture is globally appreciated, sustainably preserved, and celebrated by future generations.
